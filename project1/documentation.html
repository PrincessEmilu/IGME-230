Documentation

Ideas

When considering the design of this website, I wanted to make sure that the webstie was designed to draw attention to content. Therefor, I felt like the design needs to be simple and easy to navigate above all else.

Research

Before starting my sketches, I looked over popular game pages on itch.io to see if pages follwed a standard and how different content-creators tried to differentiate their page design. For what I saw, it seemed as though most personality on a project page came from color schemes and images. The page layouts were all relatively simple without over-the top visual effects.

I also looked at the websites for the popular indie games Undertale and Deltarune, which both followed a similar pattern of content arrangement, albeit with more creative flair within the content.
